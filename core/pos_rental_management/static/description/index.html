Now make your products rentastic in POS!!

Renting things nowadays is common in metropolitan cities. People rent their stuff and return them when used. Bring your rental business on your POS front end with Odoo POS Rental Management Module. It allows POS users to place the order of the rent based products on POS. The module adds a feature on your POS through which you can rent products to your customers. The products are published as Rental to the POS users. The POS users can place the rental orders for the customers and lease the products for desired/available time period. Being the admin, you can view, manage and renew all their rental orders from the Odoo backend.

Renting service - A new rage in POS!!

Rental products and services are becoming one of the popular services with contemporary as they offer a frivolous choice of using the products without the precedent of buying them. It mainly provides the ease of obtaining the products on rent and using them for the specified time period. And once the product is used, you can easily return them rather than purchasing it on installments with interest. If you are starting to rent products and services in POS, Odoo POS Rental Management Module is the perfect solution to manage your resources and orders in Odoo.

Detailed Features List


Manage Your Rental Business In Odoo POS

  • The module provides the structure to manage rental business from POS.
  • The POS users can rent and track products in running POS Session.


Create Rental Products And Manage Their Prices In POS

  • You can now create rental products in Odoo. The pricing of the rental products is managed on a daily and weekly basis.
  • You can choose simple pricing mechanisms for products.


Set Rental Tenures And Their Prices

  • Manage prices of rental products using appropriate UOM.
  • You can create Uom such as daily, weekly, monthly, etc for your products.
  • Use the Uom to set rental prices in your products.


Add Security Amount To Each Rental Product

  • A security amount can also be added to each product.
  • This amount is refundable when the product is returned.


Different Types Of Tenures To Place Rental Orders

  • You can create and save standard tenures for each product in POS.
  • You can then use these tenures while creating rental orders for the customers.
  • You can choose the standard tenure while creating the rental order.
