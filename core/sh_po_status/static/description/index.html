Purchase Shipment And Bill Status

This module is useful to get the status of shipment and bill of purchase orders. Easily filters purchase orders with fully shipped, partial shipped, paid, partially paid.

How it works? Click here to watch video

Features

Easy to find fully shipped, partial shipped shipments and fully paid or partially paid bills.

Easy to filter fully shipped purchase orders.

Easy to filter partial shipped purchase orders.

Easy to filter fully paid bills of purchase orders.

Easy to filter partially paid bills of purchase orders.

This app is compatible with Odoo's Multi-Company Feature.
